FERRARI F40

€1,500,000

Experience the thrill of owning a true icon of automotive history with the **Ferrari F40**—a masterpiece designed to ignite your passion for speed and luxury. As the last Ferrari personally approved by Enzo Ferrari himself, the F40 is more than just a car; it’s a legacy of performance, craftsmanship, and exclusivity. Beneath its striking, aerodynamic body lies a twin-turbocharged V8 engine that delivers an exhilarating 471 horsepower, launching you from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.8 seconds. With its lightweight design, raw power, and unmistakable presence, the F40 isn’t just a drive—it’s an experience that commands attention at every turn. This is your chance to own a timeless piece of Ferrari heritage, built for those who crave the ultimate driving adventure.

Specs

Engine: 2.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8
Horsepower: 471 hp @ 7,000 rpm
Torque: 426 lb-ft @ 4,000 rpm

Top Speed: 201 mph (324 km/h)
0-60 mph: 3.8 seconds
Transmission: 5-speed manual
Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)

Weight: 2,425 lbs (1,100 kg)
Chassis: Carbon fiber, Kevlar, and aluminum construction
Production Years: 1987–1992
