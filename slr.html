Mercedes-Mclaren SLR

€700,000

Mercedes-Benz’s SLR McLaren is a grand tourer that everycar enthusiast should be familiar with, it is quite easily one of the most historically important cars in the modern automotive era. This legendary car was manufactured from 2003 to 2010 as a joint project between McLaren and Mercedes-Benz, fusing the two company’s visions together. The design elements such as broad long curves over the doors, the signature open climbing kind of doors, and the mouth-like expanded grill were all inspired by the original attraction, the 300 SLR from the 1950s. The extravagant body has carbon fiber which along with its astonishing style, also maximizes the vehicle’s performance. What's even more impressive is its horsepower; the car boasts of a 5.4-litre hand-crafted V8 supercharged engine that generates more than 617 horsepower and the car can reach a maximum speed of around 208 mph. The SLR comes in a variety of forms starting from the original roadster form to the limited 722 editiion. McLaren SLR has created its own special place in the automotive market where exclusivity, luxury and speed meet.
